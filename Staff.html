Welcome to Zhiping Shi Dance

Zhiping Shi Dance is a professional ballet training institution under the direction of Zhiping Shi. The curriculum is unique, specially designed with Russian ballet style influence. The School offers graded classes from pre-ballet to pre-professional training to match each student's interests. Chinese dance classes are also offered. The School also accepts new students based on age, and level. Please contact us for more information.

史芝萍舞蹈学校教授专业芭蕾舞和中国民间舞。学校的课程是以北京舞蹈学院和俄国芭蕾体系为基础，凭着多年的教学经验针对湾区的学生所特别设计的。课程分不同的等级，从四岁启蒙班到专业演员预备班。

学校每年都有一次大型的学生公演。每位学生都有机会展现他们的舞蹈才能和学习成果。有时学生们也参加社区的表演活动。

学校根据年龄和程度接受插班生。有兴趣者请与我们联系。

Annual Showcase

Zhiping Shi Dance offers a major theater performance every year. Every student gets an opportunity to showcase their talents and what they have accomplished in the past year. Sometimes the students are also invited to perform and participate in various community events.
